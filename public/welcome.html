Recurly JS Welcome Page

Introduction

Welcome to Recurly JS! Our interactive platform is designed to show you just how easy it is to integrate with our Recurly.js API. Before proceeding, be sure to follow all instructions on our GitHub repository to ensure your plans populate properly.

Welcome Page

Now that you're ready to get started, let's make your experience as seamless as possible. We want you to feel at home, so we've given you a few customization options on our welcome page. Choose a background color and secondary color to fill in sections of the webpage. You can also add your brand name, brand message, and logo to truly make it your own.

Plans Plans Plans

On the plans page, you'll find a list of plans that you've created in your Recurly domain, including pricing, plan name, and plan code. Our platform is API-friendly, so you can easily integrate it into your existing system.

Checkout

Moving on to the checkout page, we make it easy for your customers to enter their relevant details for purchasing a subscription. When the payment form is submitted, Recurly.js sends the payment information to be encrypted and stored securely. We provide you with an authorization token to complete the subscription process using our API, which means you never have to handle any sensitive information, and your PCI scope is significantly reduced. Plus, we offer several payment methods and 3DS compatibility (if you use the correct payment methods).

Success

Finally, our success page confirms that the user has successfully subscribed to their selected plan. It's like a confirmation message you would display on your own website. Feel free to check your Recurly domain to see the new customer and their details in your system. Wow!

Final words

We hope you enjoy using Recurly JS and that it helps streamline your subscription process. If you have any questions, don't hesitate to reach out to our support team. Happy integrating!
